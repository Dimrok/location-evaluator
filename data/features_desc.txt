📊 SCORING METRICS:
------------------------------
🎯 ATTRACTIVENESS_SCORE
   Formula: 30% food/entertainment + 25% services + 25% parks + 20% business
   Components: restaurants + hotels + attractions + museums + banks + pharmacy + parks + business_centers

🏪 COMPETITION_SCORE
   Formula: 70% shoe shops + 30% total shops
   Components: shops_shoes + shops_total

🚇 ACCESSIBILITY_SCORE
   Formula: 40% metro + 30% walkability + 20% bus + 10% parking
   Components: metro_station + walkability_score + bus_stop + parking

👟 SUITABILITY_SCORE
   Formula: 40% low competition + 30% retail environment + 20% accessibility + 10% customer base
   Components: (100-competition) + shops_total + restaurants + accessibility + residential_buildings + business_centers

🌍 GLOBAL_SCORE
   Formula: Average of all 4 scores
   Components: (attractiveness + competition + accessibility + suitability) / 4
